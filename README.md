# Samatva: Post-Diagnosis Clarity System for Women

## From Confusion to Balance

### 🎯 About Samatva

Samatva is a comprehensive digital health platform designed to empower women by providing structured guidance, personalized insights, and expert-backed recommendations during their health recovery journey. Named from the Sanskrit word meaning "balance" or "equilibrium," Samatva bridges the critical gap between medical diagnosis and patient understanding.

---

## 🤔 The Problem

Post-diagnosis is often a challenging and confusing time for women. After receiving a diagnosis, patients frequently struggle with:
- **Information overload** without clear guidance on what to prioritize
- **Lack of structured follow-up** to track symptoms and recovery progress
- **Limited access** to expert insights and personalized recommendations
- **Emotional uncertainty** about the path to recovery and lifestyle adjustments
- **Communication gaps** between patients and healthcare providers

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Samatva transforms post-diagnosis confusion into clarity through a user-centric, pattern-based system that:

1. **Weekly Check-ins** - Simple, structured weekly assessments to monitor health status
2. **Pattern Detection** - Advanced algorithms identify trends and patterns in health data
3. **Insight Generation** - Actionable, personalized insights based on individual patterns
4. **Expert Guidance Hub** - Access to doctor-verified recommendations and educational resources
5. **Simple Recommendations** - Clear, easy-to-follow health and lifestyle guidance tailored to each user

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 📋 **Personalized Health Tracking** - Weekly wellness check-ins customized to individual diagnosis
